Our Team

Background of Our Team

We are a team of forensic psychology students from the University of Rwanda (UR), Musanze Campus, who created a website focused on our research into people's behavior. By integrating computer science, we aim to enhance forensic investigations and support the criminal justice system.

Using data analytics and machine learning, we developed tools to analyze behavior and improve criminal profiling. Our approach helps with crime detection and providing better support for victims.

Through our website and technical expertise, we offer digital platforms to streamline research on behavior, demonstrating how technology can transform forensic psychology practices.

Our Research

Our Research

Our research focuses on advancing the understanding of forensic psychology, particularly the factors that influence crime reporting and victim behavior. By examining trauma response, decision-making, and societal perceptions, we aim to address challenges faced by victims in underreported crimes like rape. Our work seeks to inform policies, improve support systems, and promote a more empathetic legal environment.
